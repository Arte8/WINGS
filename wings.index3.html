W.I.N.G.S.

Wellness Information Navigating Great Services

-a friend in need is a friend indeed..

This community resource directory is designed to connect individuals and families across Ontario with trusted support services, essential government programs, and local organizations that are here to help. Whether you’re looking for housing assistance, food support, mental health services, employment resources, or community programs, this directory brings together reliable information in one accessible place. Our goal is to make it easier to find the help you need—quickly, clearly, and without barriers. Built with care for the diverse communities of Toronto and beyond, this platform highlights both government services and grassroots initiatives that support people during challenging times. We recognize that navigating support systems can feel overwhelming, so this directory is organized to guide you toward meaningful assistance and opportunities for stability, growth, and connection. No matter your situation, you are not alone—help is available, and this resource is here to point you in the right direction.

Brought to you with thanks from Ontario Shores, Nicole and all who wear the wings.
